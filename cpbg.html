Woodraptors
Various types - Frenzied, Young, Normal
Frenized has higher attack speed and damage
Young and Normal are the easiest of the 3

Abilities
Leap : Large AOE and brief stun to a targetted area
Move away from the area
Roar : Channeled frontal cone AOE ability that does damage and debuffs a targetted player, often tries to rotate 180 degrees.
Run around to avoid
Tail Swipe : 270 degree arc attack from the bosses back right side to front cone, deals damage and stuns
Tank - take the hit. Can be avoided but the damage isn't severe
Tactics

Melee and Ranged DPS stack behind the boss, to the left of his tail - on his left leg to avoid talk swipe
Healer stack with melee and ranged
Tank (even in T2) can face take everything with a dedicated healer present.

Wooden Deer

Abilities
Ground Stomp : Large AOE and knock back in a large frontal cone starting from his hind legs. Can target ANY player that contributed to his aggro.
Move away from the front and sides of him
Charge : Immediately after the Ground Stomp ability the Deer charges straight forward to the highest threat. Summons 1 dendroid per target hit
Run left/right to avoid
Call of the Wild : Channelled ability to call 1 dendroid per player (or enemy in general, includes totems/clones etc). Also calls nearby camps if within range.
Dendroid's don't have much health but can overwhelm the healer if left unchecked. If summoned, dps quickly nuke them.
Rend Skin : If hit by the ground stomp, applies a bleed to the targets hit and causes the next attack to crit
Avoid the ground stomp, be wary of the amount of auto attacks the boss has taken. Clenase if needed

Tactics
Every 4th attack will be the ground stomp. Tank needs to count these and act accordingly.
Everyone should back off after the third hit in anticipation of the stomp.
Melee DPS stack behind the boss, to avoid the ground stomp but be ready to move.
Healer stack with and ranged. Be aware the boss could run towards you to try and stomp you.
After the ground stomp, the charge will come immediately to everyone must be aware of the bosses telegraphed charge. Just dash left or right to avoid.

Dendroid Guard

Abilities
Ground Pound : Large AOE in a circle around the boss. Hard to dodge as melee dps, tank will get hit.
DPS dash/charge/leap whatever ability you have to avoid. Doesnt deal tremendous damage, can easily be healed through
Causes 2 buffs, slow and reduced armour. Purge before stacks get too high.
Flora Spore Seed : Tosses a seed that deals damage on impact and spawns a dendroid twig
Run left/right to avoid as ranged, melee try to dodge if possible
Seed Spawn : Upon landing the seed spawn will become a dendroid twig.
Dendroid's don't have much health but can overwhelm the healer if left unchecked. When summoned, dps quickly nuke them.

Tactics
Boss moves around alot and does the slam every few seconds.
After the slam, the boss will throw a Flora Spore Seed at someone in the party, avoid the ground telegraph and deal with the add. Everyone should try to remain behind the boss.

Healer stack with and ranged. Be aware the boss could target you for teh Flora Spore Seed
